Takeaways

  • Most sites “Meet Expectations”, indicating strong baseline alignment with state standards.

  • Few sites “Exceed Expectations”, showing high performance is hard to achieve under VQB5.

  • “Needs Support” ratings are rare but more visible in Family Day Homes and Public Schools, pointing to targeted challenges.

  • Centers have the highest volume and largely meet expectations—likely due to more structured environments.

  • Regional differences suggest unequal access to quality improvement supports.
